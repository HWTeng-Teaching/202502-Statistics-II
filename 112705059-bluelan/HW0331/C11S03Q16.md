### Solution

### a. 計算最小顯著差異 (ω)

$$\omega = q_{0.01}(4,16) \times \sqrt{\frac{MSE}{n}}$$

1. **查表取得q臨界值**：  
   - $q_{0.01}(4,16) = 5.19$ (來自Studentized range分布表)

   $\sqrt{\frac{41.25}{5}} = 2.872$
   
   $\omega = 5.19 \times 2.872 = 14.91$
