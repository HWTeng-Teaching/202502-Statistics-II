## Solution

$$
t = \frac{\bar{X} - \mu}{S / \sqrt{N}} = \frac{69.7 - 74}{16 / \sqrt{25}} = \frac{-4.3}{3.2} \approx -1.34
$$

Degrees of freedom df = 24

From the t-table, for $\alpha = 0.05$ in a two-tailed test:

$$
t_{0.025,24} \approx \pm 2.064
$$

Since $$|t| = 1.34 < 2.064$$
, we fail to reject $H_0$.
