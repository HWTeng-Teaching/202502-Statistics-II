## Solution

### a.

According to data:

$n=17, \bar{x} = 31.0, s= 6.2$
\text{set } $\alpha = 0.05$

Hypothesis Test:

$$
H_0: \mu=22.0 
$$

$$
H_1: \mu>22.0
$$

$$
\text{Set } \alpha = 0.05
$$

$t_{STAT} = \frac{\bar{x} - \mu}{s/\sqrt{n}} \sim t_{n-1}$
$t^* = \frac{\bar{x} - \mu}{s/\sqrt{n}}$

**T-Value Analysis:**

$$
t_{STAT} \sim t_{n-1}
$$

$$
T-value = 1.64
$$

$$
df = n - 1 = 27 - 1 = 26
$$

$$
t_{26, 0.025} = 2.056
$$

$$
\text{Rejection region: } t < -2.056 \text{ or } t > 2.056
$$

Since t = 1.64 < 2.056, we do not reject $H_0$.
