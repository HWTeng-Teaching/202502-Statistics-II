## Solution

Stimuli 1 : $\bar{X}_1 = 1.875, s_1^2 = 0.696$
Stimuli 2 : $\bar{X}_2 = 2.625, s_2^2 = 0.839$
By Rule of Thumb, $\frac{larger \ s^2}{smaller \ s^2} = \frac{0.839}{0.696} \approx 1.205 < 3$
