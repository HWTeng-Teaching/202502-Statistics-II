## Solution of b

$H_a: \rho_s < 0$  
$\alpha = 0.05 \quad n = 12$  
$r = 0.497$  
Thus, the rejection region is $\\{r: r \leq -0.497\\}$  
Since $-0.5874 < -0.497$, $H_0$ is rejected i.e. there is sufficient evidence to indicate a negative rank correlation between rating and distance.
