## Solution of a

The least-squares regression line is: $\hat{y} = a + bx$  

where $b = \frac{S_{xy}}{S_{xx}} = \frac{\sum x_iy_i - n \bar{x} \bar{y}}{\sum x_i^2 - n \bar{x}^2} = \frac{14922.41-10*26.49\*27.2}{14935.37-10\*701.7201} \approx 0.9746$
