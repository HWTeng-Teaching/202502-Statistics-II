$H_0: D_1 = D_2$  
$H_a: D_1 \ne D_2$  

$n_1 = n_2 = 9$

$T_1 = 15 + 11 + 18 + 10 + 14 + 12 + 17 + 16 + 13 = 126$

$T_1^* = n_1(n_1 + n_2 + 1) - T_1 = 9(9 + 9 + 1) - 126 = 45$

查表臨界值：$62$  

$\min(T_1, T_1^*) = \min(126, 45) = 45 < 62$

$\Rightarrow$ 拒絕 $H_0$

$\Rightarrow$ There is difference between $D_1$ and $D_2$
