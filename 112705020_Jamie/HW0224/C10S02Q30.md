## a. Hypothesis Test 
**Null Hypothesis (Ho):**   
μ >= 25  

**Alternative Hypothesis (Ha):**  
μ < 25  

### Test Statistics:
$\ T= \frac{\bar{x} - \mu_0}{S/ \sqrt{n}} = \frac{20.3 - 25}{5/ \sqrt{21}} = -4.31 \$

t-table:  

$\ -t_{20,0.05} = -1.725 \$

Since:  

-4.31 < -1.725

### Conclusion:
$\\Rightarrow \text{Reject }H_0 \$
