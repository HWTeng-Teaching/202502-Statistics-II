$\ H_0: D_1=D_2 \$

$\ H_a:D_1 \ne D_2 \$

$\ T_1=15+11+18+10+14+12+17+16+13 =126 \$

$\ T_1^*=n_1+(n_1+n_2+1)-T_1=9(9+9+1)-126 =45 \$

$\ T_{9,9} = 62 \$

$\ min(T_1,T_1^*)=min(126,45) = 45 \$

$\ \because 45<62 \$

$\therefore$ reject $H_0$

$\Rightarrow$ there is siginificant difference between $D_1$ and $D_2$
