$\ S_{xy} = \sum x_i y_i - \frac{\sum x_i \times \sum y_i}{n}=221414 - \frac{1328 \times 1331}{8} = 468 \$

$\ S_{yy} = \sum {y_i}^2 - \frac{(\sum y_i)^2}{n} = 224877-\frac{1331^2}{8} = 431.875 \$
