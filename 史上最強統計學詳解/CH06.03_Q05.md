Central limit theorem

Suppose $X_1$, $\ldots, X_n\sim X$, where $X$ has mean $\mu$ and standard deviation $\sigma$.
Define sample mean as $\bar{X}=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n X_i$. 

Then, 

$\frac{\bar{X}-\mu}{\sigma/\sqrt{n}}\rightarrow Z$.
