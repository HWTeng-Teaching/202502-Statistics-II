### Solution

$$ \text{Least-squares regression line } $$
$$ \hat{y} = a + bx $$
$$ \text{Find b: } $$
$$ S_{xy} = \sum x_iy_i - \frac{(\sum x_i) (\sum y_i)}{n}$$
$$ S_{xy} = 14922.41 - \frac{ (264.9) (272) }{10} = 7717.13 $$
