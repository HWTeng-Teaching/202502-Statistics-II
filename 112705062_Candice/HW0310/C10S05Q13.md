### Q13
#### a.

$\bar{x} = 352.5$

$s = 1.915$

$H_0: \sigma^2 = 0.49$

$H_a: \sigma^2 > 0.49$

$\chi^2 = \frac{(n-1) s^2}{\sigma_0^2} = \frac{(4-1) \times 1.915^2}{0.49} = 22.452$

$\chi^2_{0.05,3} = 7.815$

Since 22.452 is larger than 7.815, we reject $H_0$.
